About Me

The Photographer Behind the Lens

I'm Siraj Munir — a sports photographer with a passion for capturing the raw, unscripted energy of athletic competition. Whether it's the thud of a football being struck, or the split-second pivot of a floorball player, I'm there to preserve it forever.

My approach is simple: get as close to the action as possible and let the sport speak for itself. Every frame is intentional, every edit is minimal — because the real magic happens on the court and on the pitch.
